Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. It's a comforting and hearty dish that's perfect for a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) of ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups (475ml) of tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups (475ml) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups (710ml) of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. While the sauce is simmering, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese and egg until well combined.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of ricotta mixture, a layer of mozzarella cheese, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
