Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy and indulgent Italian-American pasta dish. It combines tender chicken with a rich Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. This recipe is a delightful comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (225g) of fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup (115g) of butter
  • 1 cup (240ml) of heavy cream
  • 1 cup (90g) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat and cook the chicken until it's no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and slice it into thin strips.
  2. In the same skillet, melt the butter and add the minced garlic. Cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Cook, stirring, until the sauce thickens and the cheese is fully melted.
  4. Cook the fettuccine pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Add the cooked chicken and cooked pasta to the Alfredo sauce. Toss everything together until it's well coated.
  6. Serve the Chicken Alfredo, garnished with chopped fresh parsley. Enjoy this creamy delight!
